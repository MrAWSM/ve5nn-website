About RARA

Who We Are

The Regina Amateur Radio Association Inc. (RARA) is a membership-based club of amateur radio enthusiasts based in Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada. We operate under the callsign VE5NN.

Our members range from newly licensed hams to seasoned operators with decades of experience. We welcome anyone with an interest in amateur radio — whether you're preparing for your licence exam, building antennas, chasing DX, or just enjoying the community.

RARA endorses membership in Radio Amateurs of Canada (RAC) and supports the DX Code of Conduct.

Membership

Interested in joining RARA? We welcome licensed amateur radio operators and those interested in learning more about the hobby.

RARA endorses membership in Radio Amateurs of Canada — Canada's national amateur radio organization. RAC provides member benefits including QSL bureau service, insurance coverage for club activities, and advocacy for amateur radio in Canada.
